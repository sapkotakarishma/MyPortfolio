About

I am an applied data scientist with interdisciplinary training spanning clinical practice, public health systems, and computational analytics. My work focuses on building data-driven solutions that translate complex datasets into interpretable insights for real-world decision making.


Across research and applied environments, I have worked with surveillance data, operational systems, behavioral datasets, and unstructured text. My projects combine statistical modeling, machine learning, and data engineering to support decision-making in both research and industry contexts.


I am particularly interested in the design of intelligent data systems that bridge machine learning, human interpretation, and organizational decision processes across diverse domains.

Selected Projects

Population Disease Surveillance Analytics

Developed analytical models supporting elimination programs for neglected tropical diseases including Lymphatic Filariasis, Visceral Leishmaniasis, and Malaria. The work integrated surveillance datasets and generated municipality-level risk indicators used for program planning and intervention prioritization.

Methods: statistical modeling, epidemiological indicators, program monitoring analytics.

Operational Analytics for Pharmacy Systems

Designed interactive analytics dashboards integrating operational pharmacy datasets including verification workload, medication safety events, inventory patterns, and revenue metrics to support operational decision making and quality improvement.

Tools: Power BI, SQL pipelines, data modeling.

Emotion Detection in Social Media Data

Evaluated transformer-based NLP models and lexicon approaches for emotion classification in social media datasets, exploring trade-offs between interpretability and predictive performance in behavioral analytics.

Methods: NLP pipelines, DistilRoBERTa, sentiment and emotion analysis.

Financial Fraud Detection Modeling

Implemented predictive models for fraud detection in highly imbalanced transactional datasets. The analysis focused on rare event detection and model evaluation using ROC/AUC and sensitivity metrics.

Methods: logistic regression, anomaly detection, model evaluation.
