About

I’m an applied data scientist with a background that spans clinical nursing, public health programs, and advanced analytics. I’ve worked on surveillance systems, pharmacy operations, mixed‑methods research, and machine learning projects that sit close to real decisions and real people.

I enjoy designing models, dashboards, and workflows that are technically sound but also readable, explainable, and grounded in context. I’m especially interested in work that blends health systems, behavioral data, and intelligent tools.

Projects

NTD & Malaria Surveillance Analytics

Pharmacy Operations & Medication Safety Dashboards

Power BI dashboards for verification workload, inventory, revenue, and medication safety events, integrating data from multiple systems to support continuous quality improvement.

Emotion Classification on Reddit

Comparative analysis of lexicon‑based methods and DistilRoBERTa for emotion classification, exploring trade‑offs between interpretability and performance for digital mental health contexts.

Credit Card Fraud Detection

Logistic regression on an imbalanced dataset, focusing on ROC/AUC and sensitivity to rare events to demonstrate applied ML for risk detection.
