About Me

I am a population health and applied data scientist who bridges clinical practice, public health systems, and advanced analytics. My work spans neglected tropical disease elimination, malaria surveillance, basic health service coverage, pharmacy operations, and digital behavioral health. I design interpretable models, interactive dashboards, and mixed‑methods research frameworks that translate complex data into actionable insights for decision‑makers.

My experience includes provincial‑level NTD analytics, municipal health profiling, medication safety surveillance, predictive modeling (heart disease, fraud detection), and NLP for sentiment and emotion classification. I am particularly interested in explainable and agentic AI systems that support safe, equitable, and context‑aware decision‑making in health and operational settings.

Selected Projects & Analytics Work

📊 NTD & Malaria Surveillance Analytics

Designed and implemented provincial‑level analytics for Lymphatic Filariasis, Visceral Leishmaniasis, and Malaria elimination programs. Consolidated fragmented municipal datasets, monitored coverage and completion rates, and identified high‑risk areas to guide targeted interventions and resource allocation.

💊 Pharmacy Operations & Medication Safety Dashboards

Built enterprise‑level Power BI dashboards for pharmacy operations, including verification workload, order volume, inventory levels, revenue trends, and medication safety interventions. Developed a centralized error monitoring system integrating Microsoft Forms and Power BI to track near‑misses and error types, supporting continuous quality improvement.

🧠 Emotion Classification in Social Media Content

Conducted a comparative study on 37,000+ Reddit posts using rule‑based lexicons and a DistilRoBERTa transformer model. Evaluated performance across Ekman’s and Plutchik’s emotion taxonomies, highlighting trade‑offs between accuracy, recall, and interpretability for digital mental health applications.

💳 Credit Card Fraud Detection

Developed a logistic regression model on a highly imbalanced Kaggle dataset to distinguish fraudulent from non‑fraudulent transactions. Evaluated performance using ROC and AUC, achieving high discrimination while maintaining sensitivity to rare events.

📈 Interactive Data Visualization & Network Analysis

Built interactive visualizations and network analyses, including Facebook network community detection (Louvain, Girvan‑Newman) and sentiment analysis on Yelp, IMDB, and Amazon reviews using SVM, Naive Bayes, and Logistic Regression. Developed Observable notebooks for exploratory analysis and narrative data storytelling.
